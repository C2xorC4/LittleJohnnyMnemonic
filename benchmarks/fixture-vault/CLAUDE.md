# Benchmark Fixture Vault

Public-safe excerpt vault for LJM comparative benchmarks. Content is drawn from
**ingested published sources** (OOTM, GKE) and cross-domain semantic synthesis.

**Included:** book/framework knowledge, attributed excerpts, cross-book bridges.

**Excluded:** SSH paths, hostnames, IPs, employer data, application inventories,
or anything that could aid targeting of operator networks or machines.

Do not point production hooks at this directory.
